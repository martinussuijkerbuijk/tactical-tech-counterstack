# Amnesty International, "Chile: Eyes on the Crisis" (2020)

**Source type:** Human rights report  
**Publisher:** Amnesty International  
**Date:** 2020  
**Access:** Publicly available at amnesty.org

## Summary
This report documents eye injuries and excessive force by Carabineros de Chile during the 2019 Estallido Social protests. It records testimony from victims, medical personnel, and legal observers regarding the use of rubber bullets, tear gas, and kinetic impact projectiles against protesters.

## Relevant to lithium-triangle
The report documents the fast violence deployed against protesters demanding structural change, including demands for water rights and territorial autonomy. The militarized response to the 2019 protests is the terminal point in the chain of violence that begins with the 1981 Water Code.
