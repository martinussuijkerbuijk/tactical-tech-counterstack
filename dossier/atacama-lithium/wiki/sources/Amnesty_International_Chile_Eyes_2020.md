## Argument
Amnesty International documents eye injuries and excessive force by Carabineros de Chile during the 2019 Estallido Social protests, recording testimony from victims and medical personnel regarding the use of rubber bullets and kinetic impact projectiles.

## Why it matters to the Substrate Collective
This source provides documented evidence of fast violence deployed against civilians protesting structural inequality and territorial dispossession. It connects the terminal violence of state repression to the foundational violence of the 1981 Water Code.

## Key concepts introduced
- [[fast-violence]] — The sudden deployment of military and police force against protesters.
- [[structural-violence]] — The protests demanded reversal of the privatization regime inaugurated by the Pinochet dictatorship.
- [[necropolitics]] — The state decides who may live and who may die through the calibration of force.
